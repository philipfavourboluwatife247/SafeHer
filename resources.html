Resources & Support Directory

When a girl decides to ask for help, the last thing she should face is confusion.

This directory brings together trusted Nigerian NGOs, mental health support services, and legal aid organisations, verified and organised so help is easy to find. Use the sections below to find emergency lines, counselling, legal support, or shelter.
