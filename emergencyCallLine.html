If you or someone you know is at risk, you are not alone. These lines and organisations can help you get immediate support and protection — confidentially and safely.

In Immediate Danger?

If you are in immediate physical danger, call your local emergency services (e.g., 112, 911) or move to a safer location if possible. If you cannot speak, try to text, or use a nearby Wi-Fi enabled device to seek help.
