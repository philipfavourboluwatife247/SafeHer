Women's Rights: What Every Girl Should Know

Women's rights are the basic rights and freedoms every woman and girl is entitled to simply because she is human. They protect her safety, dignity, freedom, and opportunities in life.

1. Right to Education

Every girl has the right to:

  • Go to school
  • Learn and grow
  • Access quality education
  • Build a future
  • Pursue higher education
  • Choose her career

2. Right to Safety

Girls and women have the right to live free from:

  • Domestic violence
  • Sexual harassment
  • Rape
  • Child marriage
  • Trafficking

3. Right to Healthcare

Access to comprehensive healthcare including:

  • Reproductive health
  • Menstrual health
  • Mental health

4. Right to Equal Opportunities

Equal access to:

  • Education
  • Skills training
  • Jobs
  • Equal pay
  • Leadership roles

5. Right to Speak and Be Heard

Every woman has the right to:

  • Express opinions
  • Report abuse
  • Participate in decisions
  • Choose career paths
  • Say NO to discomfort

7. Right to Justice

Access to legal protection:

  • Right to report abuse
  • Right to protection
  • Right to fair hearing
  • Right to justice
